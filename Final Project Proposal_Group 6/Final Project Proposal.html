Background and Motivation:


We all have been to NYC and it is always a problem to find a nice place to stay since there are so many people in NYC. On the other hand, if we can invest a property in NYC and rent it on Airbnb, is that going to be profitable?

Project Objectives:


We are going to identify the most profitable area (zip code) to invest for a real estate company that has a niche in purchasing properties to rent out short-term or long-term as part of their business model specifically within New York City.

Data:


We are going to get data from Airbnb and Zillow
https://www.zillow.com/research/data/

Data Processing:


We are going to do a data cleaning for both datasets. We are going to get the house price from Zillow data and get the expected profit from Airbnb data. Since the data may have missing values or at a different timeline, we are going to do a data quality check and fix the problem if they have. Data quality includes 7 dimensions: conformity, uniqueness, consistency, accuracy, completeness, relevance, and timeliness.
