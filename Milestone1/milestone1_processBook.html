Project Objectives:


We will identify the most profitable area (zip code) to invest for a real estate company that has a niche in purchasing properties to rent out short-term or long-term as part of their business model specifically within New York City.

Data:


We got data from Airbnb and Zillow

Data Processing:


We did a data cleaning for both datasets. We got the house price from Zillow data and got the expected profit from Airbnb data. Since the data may have missing values or at a different timeline, we did a data quality check and fix the problem if they have. Data quality includes 7 dimensions: conformity, uniqueness, consistency, accuracy, completeness, relevance, and timeliness.
